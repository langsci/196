<p>Our books are free for readers and authors. We rely on a distributed funding model, where research institutions worldwide contribute towards the financing of our publications, similar to crowdfunding.</p>

Read more about our cooperation <a title="Cooperation between Language Science Press and Knowledge Unlatched" href="http://www.knowledgeunlatched.org/2017/03/ku-launches-language-science-press/" target="_blank">here</a>and on our <a title="blogpost cooperation knowledge unlatched" href="http://userblogs.fu-berlin.de/langsci-press/2017/04/18/future-of-language-science-press/">blog post</a>.</p>
